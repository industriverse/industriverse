# Industriverse Sales One-Pagers: The 10 Offers
> **Commercial Strategy & Product Packaging**
> *Date: December 2025*

This document outlines the 10 core commercial offerings for Industriverse, ranging from Sovereign Infrastructure to Protocol Licensing.

---

## 1. Sovereign Stack — National-Scale AI Infrastructure
**Value Proposition**: "Deploy Industriverse as a sovereign AI operating system — enabling governments to simulate, govern, and evolve their critical infrastructure."
*   **Target**: Governments, Defense, Critical Infrastructure.
*   **Key Feature**: Air-gapped, fully owned, no foreign dependencies.
*   **Price**: $5M–$25M.

## 2. DFY Platform-as-a-Product (PaaP)
**Value Proposition**: "Deploy your own Industriverse instance pre-loaded with capsule intelligence. Go live in days, not months."
*   **Target**: Enterprises, Smart Cities.
*   **Key Feature**: Turnkey deployment, vertical-specific capsules.
*   **Price**: $300K–$1.5M/year.

## 3. White-Labeled Intelligence Fabric
**Value Proposition**: "Offer a next-gen agentic intelligence platform under your own brand."
*   **Target**: SIs, Consultancies, Telcos.
*   **Key Feature**: Custom branding, resale rights.
*   **Price**: $750K/year base.

## 4. Capsule Intelligence Subscription
**Value Proposition**: "Subscribe to a constantly evolving ecosystem of industrial capsules."
*   **Target**: Operations Teams, R&D.
*   **Key Feature**: SaaS-like access to agent capabilities.
*   **Price**: $5K–$50K/month.

## 5. Simulation-as-a-Service (SaaS)
**Value Proposition**: "Simulate your industry’s future before you commit."
*   **Target**: R&D, Policy Makers.
*   **Key Feature**: Cloud-hosted simulation sandbox.
*   **Price**: $2K–$50K/month.

## 6. Digital Twin Exchange (DTX)
**Value Proposition**: "Monetize the Machines of the Future. The GitHub for industrial reality models."
*   **Target**: OEMs, Engineering Firms.
*   **Key Feature**: Marketplace for certified Digital Twins.
*   **Price**: 10% Fee + Licensing.

## 7. Agent Network Orchestration Services
**Value Proposition**: "Command the Swarm. Orchestrate autonomous fleets with trust at scale."
*   **Target**: Logistics, Defense, Telecom.
*   **Key Feature**: Managing 10k+ agents with trust scoring.
*   **Price**: $5K–$100K/month.

## 8. Industriverse Academy
**Value Proposition**: "Train the next generation of agentic infrastructure engineers."
*   **Target**: Universities, Enterprises.
*   **Key Feature**: Certification paths (e.g., Capsule Engineer).
*   **Price**: $995/cert to $250K/license.

## 9. Regulatory Intelligence Toolkit
**Value Proposition**: "Trust, Proven. Explainable and auditable AI behavior."
*   **Target**: Regulated Industries (Finance, Energy).
*   **Key Feature**: ZK-proofs, immutable audit logs.
*   **Price**: $25K–$500K/year.

## 10. Industriverse-as-a-Protocol
**Value Proposition**: "The Agentic Standard. License the protocol stack to build your own world."
*   **Target**: Cloud Providers, Robotics Platforms.
*   **Key Feature**: Source code/Protocol SDK licensing.
*   **Price**: $500K–$10M.
