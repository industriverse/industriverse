## 3. The "Continuous Daemon" Connection
How our code automates our brand.

### The Pipeline
1.  **The Event**: A code module triggers (e.g., `SPI` detects a botnet, `SCDS` blocks a spy).
2.  **The Translation**: The **Content Engine** (`scripts/marketing/content_engine.py`) reads the telemetry.
3.  **The Branding**: The Engine applies the correct "Voice" and "Visual" based on the context.

### Examples

#### Scenario 1: SPI Detects a Botnet
*   **Brand**: **Empeiria Haus** (Research Discovery)
*   **Headline**: "Anomaly Detected: Non-Physical Social Harmonics."

## 4. Social Media Strategy (The "Pulse")

### X (Twitter) - The "War Room"
*   **Frequency**: High (Hourly automated updates).
*   **Content**: Real-time alerts from the Iron Dome. "Botnet detected in Brazil." "GPS Spoofing in London."
*   **Style**: Terminal screenshots, raw data, "breaking news" urgency.

### LinkedIn - The "Board Room"
*   **Frequency**: Low (Weekly deep dives).
*   **Content**: "State of the Grid" reports. Whitepaper summaries from the Paper Factory.
*   **Style**: Polished PDFs, executive summaries, thought leadership.

### Instagram/TikTok - The "Visualizer"
*   **Frequency**: Medium (Daily highlights).
*   **Content**: 3D visualizations of the Energy Atlas. "See the heat map of your city."
*   **Style**: High-aesthetic motion graphics (generated by `EvolutionDashboard.jsx` captures).
