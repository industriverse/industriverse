# North Star Vision: The Thermodynamic Operating System

## Part 1: The Vision
**Ambient Intelligence delivered through Capsule Pins**
The Industriverse is not just a platform; it is a **Thermodynamic Operating System** for the physical economy. The core interface is the **Capsule Pin**—a pervasive, ambient UI element that exists across all devices (iOS, Android, Web, Desktop, AR/VR). 
*   **Deploy Anywhere Capsules (DACs)** are the packaging layer.
*   **Three-Layer Architecture**:
    1.  **Bottom**: The Industriverse Framework (10 layers).
    2.  **Middle**: The Discovery Loop (Thermodynasty).
    3.  **Top**: Ambient Intelligence (Capsule Pins).

## Part 2: The DAC Factory
**From Hypothesis to Deployable Capsule**
The DAC Factory is the engine that transforms thermodynamic hypotheses into sovereign digital workers.
*   **32-Step Lifecycle**: From `concept` -> `simulation` -> `build` -> `deploy` -> `scale` -> `retire`.
*   **Integration**: Connects with 1180+ services via the Service Mesh.
*   **Manifest**: Every DAC has a `manifest.json` with UTID, zk-SNARK proofs, and energy signatures.

## Part 3: Service Orchestration
**The Nervous System**
*   **ASI (Autonomous Service Injector)**: Discovers and injects services (1200+ registry).
*   **TTF (Thermodynamic Tunneling Fabric)**: Energy-aware routing protocol that moves compute to where it is most thermodynamically efficient.
*   **Energy Atlas**: A temporal knowledge graph tracking the energy cost and provenance of every operation.
*   **Recursive Loop**: The 4-layer loop (Perceive, Predict, Proof, Propel) drives continuous improvement.

## Part 4: Ambient Intelligence Projection
**Ubiquitous Access**
*   **Capsule Pins**:
    *   **iOS**: Live Activities, Dynamic Island.
    *   **Android**: Bubbles, Notifications.
    *   **Web**: PWA, Floating Widgets (`<iv-capsule-pin>`).
    *   **Desktop**: System Tray, Menu Bar.
*   **Whitelabel Portals**: Enterprise clients receive their own branded instance.
*   **Dynamic Islands**: Provide continuous, non-intrusive awareness of industrial states.

## Part 5: Business Model & Client Delivery
