*   **Gossip Protocol**: When Device A detects a new "Surveillance Signature" (e.g., a specific wake-lock pattern), it broadcasts a **Zero-Knowledge Proof** of the threat to the A2A Mesh.

*   **Privacy**: No user data is shared, only the *physics of the attack*.

### 3. eSIM as the "Root of Truth"
The eSIM is the most privileged entry point on a modern device. We utilize it as the **Anchor**.
*   **Thermodynamic Identity**: The eSIM stores the device's "Energy Baseline". Any deviation is signed by the eSIM's secure enclave.
*   **Network Forensics**: The eSIM profile validates the *integrity* of the cellular connection, detecting "Stingray" (IMSI Catcher) attacks by analyzing signal timing and tower handshakes.
