# Industriverse Training Programs: The Daemon Curriculum
> **From Codebase to Competence**
> *Date: December 2025*

Based on the "Daemon Orchestration Levels" and "War Mode" features, we define the following comprehensive training tracks.

## Track 1: Certified Daemon Operator (Level 1-2)
**Target**: System Administrators, DevOps.
**Focus**: Managing Peace and Vigilance modes.
**Curriculum**:
1.  **Daemon Anatomy**: Understanding `DaemonGears` and `OrchestrationLevelManager`.
2.  **Vigilance Monitoring**: Using `DeepSCDS` and interpreting Threat Scores.
3.  **Resource Management**: Balancing Discovery vs. Optimization.
4.  **Safe Shutdown**: Standard procedures vs. Emergency Stops.

## Track 2: Growth Architect (Level 3)
**Target**: Business Strategists, Expansion Leads.
**Focus**: Managing Expansion mode.
**Curriculum**:
1.  **DAC Spawning**: Configuring `AggressiveDAC` strategies.
2.  **RCO Extraction**: Maximizing yield from `ResourceClusterObjects`.
3.  **Economic Velocity**: Tuning the `MarketMaker` for rapid capital deployment.
4.  **Scaling Logic**: When to shift from Peace to Expansion.

## Track 3: War Room Strategist (Level 4)
**Target**: Elite Operators, CISOs, "The General".
**Focus**: Managing WAR (Code Red) mode.
**Curriculum**:
1.  **The Code Red Trigger**: When to engage `WarFeatures`.
2.  **Scorched Earth Protocols**: Understanding the trade-offs of killing UI/Logs.
3.  **Offensive Operations**: Deploying `WarDACs` and `OffensiveSPI`.
4.  **Phoenix Protocol**: Managing the "Self-Destruct & Rebirth" lifecycle.
5.  **Ethical Warfare**: Rules of engagement for `LiquidityDrain` and `CounterOps`.

## Track 4: Capsule Engineer (The Builder)
**Target**: Developers.
**Focus**: Building modules that survive War Mode.
**Curriculum**:
1.  **Resilient Coding**: Writing Capsules that function without UI/Logs.
2.  **War-Ready Interfaces**: Implementing `WarMode` hooks in custom agents.
3.  **HyperLoop Safety**: Designing agents that don't crash at 10x speed.

---
**Certification Path**:
*   **Novice** -> **Operator** (Track 1)
*   **Operator** -> **Architect** (Track 2)
*   **Architect** -> **Strategist** (Track 3) -> **"The Lithographer"**
