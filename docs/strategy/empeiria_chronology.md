## 2. The Framework of Mastery (The 4 Stages)
The Daemon orchestrates a continuous cycle of learning for every asset in the Industriverse.

### Stage 1: Observation (The Ingest Loop)
*   **Action**: The system watches the human master.
*   **Data**: Egocentric Video, Telemetry, Vibration.
*   **Goal**: Capture the "Trade Secret" (the subtle wrist flick, the thermal sweet spot).
*   **Daemon State**: `COLLECTING`

### Stage 2: Imitation (The Simulation Loop)
*   **Action**: The system attempts to replicate the observed action in the Digital Twin.
*   **Data**: Physics Simulation (TGE), Collision Detection.
*   **Goal**: Validate safety and feasibility.
*   **Daemon State**: `SIMULATING`

### Stage 3: Innovation (The Optimization Loop)
*   **Action**: The system improves upon the human baseline.
*   **Data**: Thermodynamic Analysis (Entropy Oracle).
*   **Goal**: Find the "Coolest Path" (Lowest Energy, Highest Quality).
*   **Daemon State**: `OPTIMIZING`

### Stage 4: Mastery (The Crystallization Loop)
*   **Action**: The system packages the optimized skill into a **Capsule**.
*   **Data**: ZK-Proof, PSSM Weight Update.
*   **Goal**: Deploy the skill to the global fleet.
*   **Daemon State**: `PUBLISHING`
