# THE EMPEIRIA HAUS AUTONOMOUS RESEARCH ENGINE

**"The Lab that Never Sleeps"**

## 1. Core Premise
Research papers are structured summaries of observation, hypothesis, experiment, and conclusion.
Since Industriverse automates all these steps, **Empeiria Haus can generate research papers autonomously.**

## 2. The Autonomous Research Stack

### Layer 1: Ingestion (Reality Capture)
*   **Sources**: Ego2Robot, Visual Twin, EDCoC Sensors, Data Hub.
*   **Output**: Infinite raw material.

### Layer 2: Hypothesis (Idea Generation)
*   **Engines**: EBDMs, Physics Priors, TNNs, OBMI.
*   **Output**: Stable hypotheses (e.g., "Entropy reduction observed when...").

### Layer 3: Experiment (Autonomous Testing)
*   **Actions**: Sandbox runs, TNN simulations, POP overlays, A2A negotiations.
*   **Output**: Experimental data.

### Layer 4: Discovery (Result Extraction)
*   **Metrics**: Entropy reduction, energy minimization, efficiency deltas, anomalies.
*   **Output**: Tables, charts, heat maps.

### Layer 5: Narrative (Paper Writing)
*   **Engine**: `paper_generator.py`.
*   **Output**: Full papers (Abstract -> References) with Dyson Sphere visuals.

## 3. The Four Streams of Papers
1.  **Physics Papers**: Entropy-driven optimization, TNNs.
2.  **AI & Computational Papers**: EBDMs, Skill Trees, Capsule Economies.
3.  **Robotics & Autonomy Papers**: Visual Twin, Operator Shadow Twins.
4.  **Novel Industrial Intelligence Papers**: Ambient Intelligence, EDCoC Edge Compute.

## 4. The Workflow
1.  **Daemon Detects Pattern**: `research_event = True`.
2.  **EBDM Generates Hypothesis**: "Thermal drift correlates with efficiency loss."
3.  **System Runs Experiment**: Simulates condition in TNN.
4.  **System Extracts Results**: Generates entropy graphs.
5.  **Paper Assembly**: Generates PDF/Markdown.
6.  **Publishing**: Released under "Empeiria Haus Research Series".
