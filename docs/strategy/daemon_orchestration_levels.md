# Daemon Orchestration Levels: The Gears of War
> **Operationalizing "Code Red" for Brutal Competition**
> *Date: December 2025*

To handle "brutal competition," the Industriverse Daemon must be able to shift gears. We define 4 distinct levels of operation.

## The 4 Orchestration Levels (The Gears)

### Level 1: PEACE (Baseline)
*   **Focus**: Efficiency, Stability, Long-term Growth.
*   **Behavior**: Standard discovery loops, conservative resource usage, passive defense.
*   **Metric**: OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness).

### Level 2: VIGILANCE (Defensive)
*   **Focus**: Security, Integrity, Monitoring.
*   **Behavior**: Heightened SCDS scanning, SPI sentiment tracking, stricter trust scores.
*   **Metric**: Threat Detection Rate.

### Level 3: EXPANSION (Growth)
*   **Focus**: Speed, Acquisition, Market Share.
*   **Behavior**: Aggressive DAC creation, high RCO extraction, rapid capital deployment.
*   **Metric**: DAC Spawn Rate / Revenue Velocity.

### Level 4: WAR (Code Red)
*   **Focus**: Survival, Dominance, "Scorched Earth".
*   **Behavior**: Maximum resource extraction, offensive SPI, unsafe acceleration, economic warfare.
*   **Metric**: **Survival & Kill Count**.

---

## The 10 "Code Red" Features (War Mode)

When the Daemon shifts to **Level 4 (WAR)**, these 10 features activate:

1.  **OrchestrationLevelManager**: The central "Gear Shifter" that coordinates the state change.
2.  **ScorchedEarthOptimizer**: Ruthlessly kills non-essential processes (UI, logs) to free up 100% compute for defense/offense.
3.  **WarDACFactory**: Spawns "Kamikaze DACs" designed for high-risk, high-reward arbitrage.
4.  **OffensiveSPI**: Generates counter-narratives and "Noise Floods" to disrupt competitor signals.
5.  **HyperLoopAccelerator**: Disables safety checks in the Discovery Loop to 10x the speed of innovation.
6.  **ThermalOverride**: Overclocks hardware to physical limits (ignoring lifespan) for short-term compute bursts.
7.  **SovereignLockdown**: Instantly air-gaps the system, cutting all external API links except trusted encrypted channels.
8.  **LiquidityDrain**: Dumps liquid assets/tokens to destabilize the local economic environment (simulated).
9.  **PhoenixProtocol**: Automated encrypted backup to a decentralized shard, followed by a local data wipe (Self-Destruct).
10. **WarRoomFeed**: A low-latency, text-only telemetry stream for the human operator (The General).
