**"Scientific Purity meets Cosmic Mystery."**

*   **Deepest Void Black (#000000)**: The canvas of infinite potential.

*   **Interaction**: Hovering over an artifact causes it to "materialize" from wireframe to solid.
*   **Sound**: Subtle, deep hums and crystalline clicks.
