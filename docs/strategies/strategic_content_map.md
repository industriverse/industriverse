# Industriverse Strategic Content Map

## Overview

This strategic content map links Industriverse framework components, collaterals, and artifacts to specific operational, deployment, and adoption workflows. It serves as a comprehensive guide for navigating the Industriverse ecosystem, ensuring that stakeholders can quickly identify and access the resources they need for their specific use cases and scenarios.

The map is organized by primary workflows, with each section detailing the relevant components, collaterals, and artifacts needed to support that workflow. Cross-references between workflows highlight interdependencies and integration points, ensuring a holistic understanding of the Industriverse platform.

## 1. Platform Implementation Workflows

**Key Stakeholders:**
